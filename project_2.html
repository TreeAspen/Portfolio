CLT Legal AI Assistant

A one-day hackathon project that won 1st Place. My role was UI/UX and prototype design.

Goal

To design a clear and accessible interface for exploring Community Land Trust (CLT) laws across U.S. states, combining structured data tables with conversational search.

Design Focus

  • Presenting dense legal data in an intuitive format.
  • Balancing a data-driven table view with natural-language chatbot interaction.
  • Maintaining visual hierarchy and readability across modules.

Outcome

A modular system including a responsive Table View, a contextual chatbot, and a searchable state selector that simplifies navigation through complex housing legislation.
